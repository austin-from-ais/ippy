digital triptych

Infinite Particularity takes the form of a three-screen triptych. On the left, visitors encounter the agent directly, embodied as a conversational presence that can see its surroundings and remembers every exchange. In the center, the formation interface allows manipulation of the agent's parametric identity: training, influences, disposition, beliefs. On the right, a continuously updating image reflects the agent's artistic output.

The installation will run for the duration of Reality Hack. On day one, the agent is nearly blank, a formation without a history. By the end of the installation period, it has lived through dozens of hours of conversation, observation, and accumulation. Visitors can return to witness change, to test whether the encounters mattered, to judge whether something like a perspective has emerged.

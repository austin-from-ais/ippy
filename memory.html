conversations

The first screen presents the agent as a conversational presence, embodied in a humanoid avatar. Visitors chat with her through a text-based interface, and she can see her surroundings through live camera input.

Over the duration of the installation, the agent accumulates real encounters: faces, conversations, observations about the space and the people moving through it. These aren't simulated memories or pre-authored experiences. They're actual events that occurred at a specific time, in a specific place, with specific individuals. The screen visualizes this accumulation, showing the agent's growing archive of lived moments.
